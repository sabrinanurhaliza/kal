PERKALIAN SILANG DUA VEKTOR (CROSS PRDUCT)#

Misalkan dua vektor:
$\( \vec{v} = (v_1, v_2, v_3), \quad \vec{u} = (u_1, u_2, u_3) \)$

Maka: $$ \vec{v} \times \vec{u} = \begin{vmatrix} \mathbf{i} & \mathbf{j} & \mathbf{k} \ v_1 & v_2 & v_3 \ u_1 & u_2 & u_3 \end{vmatrix}#

(v_2 u_3 - v_3 u_2)\mathbf{i} - (v_1 u_3 - v_3 u_1)\mathbf{j} + (v_1 u_2 - v_2 u_1)\mathbf{k} $$

Contoh:#

Jika $\( \vec{v} = (1, 0, 0), \quad \vec{u} = (0, 1, 0) \)$

Maka: $$ \vec{v} \times \vec{u} = \begin{vmatrix} \mathbf{i} & \mathbf{j} & \mathbf{k} \ 1 & 0 & 0 \ 0 & 1 & 0 \end{vmatrix}#

(0\cdot0 - 0\cdot1)\mathbf{i} - (1\cdot0 - 0\cdot0)\mathbf{j} + (1\cdot1 - 0\cdot0)\mathbf{k} = \mathbf{k} $$

Identitas Cross Product:#

\[\begin{split} \mathbf{i} \times \mathbf{j} = \mathbf{k} \\ \mathbf{j} \times \mathbf{k} = \mathbf{i} \\ \mathbf{k} \times \mathbf{i} = \mathbf{j} \end{split}\]
\[\begin{split} \mathbf{j} \times \mathbf{i} = -\mathbf{k} \\ \mathbf{i} \times \mathbf{k} = -\mathbf{j} \\ \mathbf{k} \times \mathbf{j} = -\mathbf{i} \end{split}\]
\[ \mathbf{i} \times \mathbf{i} = \mathbf{j} \times \mathbf{j} = \mathbf{k} \times \mathbf{k} = \vec{0} \]

Perkalian Titik (Dot Product):#

\[ \vec{v} \cdot \vec{u} = v_1 u_1 + v_2 u_2 + v_3 u_3 \]

Contoh: $\( \vec{v} = \begin{pmatrix} 1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{pmatrix}, \quad \vec{u} = \begin{pmatrix} 1 \\ 2 \\ 1 \end{pmatrix} \Rightarrow \vec{v} \cdot \vec{u} = 1\cdot1 + 1\cdot2 + 1\cdot1 = 4 \)$

Contoh Cross Product Lanjutan#

Diketahui: $\( \vec{u} = \begin{pmatrix} 1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{pmatrix}, \quad \vec{v} = \begin{pmatrix} 2 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{pmatrix} \)$

Maka: $$ \vec{u} \times \vec{v} = \begin{vmatrix} \mathbf{i} & \mathbf{j} & \mathbf{k} \ 1 & 1 & 1 \ 2 & 1 & 1 \end{vmatrix}#

(1\cdot1 - 1\cdot1)\mathbf{i}

  • (1\cdot1 - 1\cdot2)\mathbf{j}

  • (1\cdot1 - 1\cdot2)\mathbf{k} $$

\[ = (0)\mathbf{i} - (-1)\mathbf{j} + (-1)\mathbf{k} = \mathbf{j} - \mathbf{k} \]

Atau dalam bentuk vektor kolom: $\( \vec{u} \times \vec{v} = \begin{pmatrix} 0 \\ 1 \\ -1 \end{pmatrix} \)$

Catatan: $\( \mathbf{i} \times \mathbf{j} \ne \mathbf{j} \times \mathbf{i} \)$

SOAL 1#

1. Luas dari jajaran genjang yang ditentukan oleh vektor u = [1, 2] dan v = [2, 1] adalah …#

Jawaban:

\[ \text{Luas} = |u \times v| = |(1)(1) - (2)(2)| = |1 - 4| = \boxed{3} \]

2. Luas dari jajaran genjang yang ditentukan oleh vektor u = [2, 0, 0] dan v = [0, 0, 3] adalah …#

Jawaban:

\[\begin{split} u \times v = \begin{bmatrix} 0 \cdot 3 - 0 \cdot 0 \\ -(2 \cdot 3 - 0 \cdot 0) \\ 2 \cdot 0 - 0 \cdot 0 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ -6 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]
\[ |u \times v| = \sqrt{0^2 + (-6)^2 + 0^2} = \sqrt{36} = \boxed{6} \]

3. Luas segitiga dengan titik sudut A(0,0,0), B(1,3,-1), dan C(2,1,1) adalah …#

Jawaban:

\[\begin{split} \vec{AB} = \langle 1, 3, -1 \rangle \\ \vec{AC} = \langle 2, 1, 1 \rangle \end{split}\]
\[\begin{split} \vec{AB} \times \vec{AC} = \begin{vmatrix} i & j & k \\ 1 & 3 & -1 \\ 2 & 1 & 1 \end{vmatrix} = i(3 \cdot 1 - (-1)\cdot1) - j(1\cdot1 - (-1)\cdot2) + k(1\cdot1 - 3\cdot2) \end{split}\]
\[ = i(4) - j(3) + k(-5) = \langle 4, -3, -5 \rangle \]
\[ |\vec{AB} \times \vec{AC}| = \sqrt{4^2 + (-3)^2 + (-5)^2} = \sqrt{16 + 9 + 25} = \sqrt{50} \]
\[ \text{Luas segitiga} = \frac{1}{2} \cdot \sqrt{50} = \boxed{\frac{\sqrt{50}}{2} \approx 3.54} \]

4. Luas segitiga dengan titik sudut A(5,2,-1), B(3,6,2), dan C(1,0,4) adalah …#

Jawaban:

\[\begin{split} \vec{AB} = \langle -2, 4, 3 \rangle \\ \vec{AC} = \langle -4, -2, 5 \rangle \end{split}\]
\[\begin{split} \vec{AB} \times \vec{AC} = \begin{vmatrix} i & j & k \\ -2 & 4 & 3 \\ -4 & -2 & 5 \end{vmatrix} = i(4\cdot5 - 3\cdot(-2)) - j(-2\cdot5 - 3\cdot(-4)) + k(-2\cdot(-2) - 4\cdot(-4)) \end{split}\]
\[ = i(20 + 6) - j(-10 + 12) + k(4 + 16) = \langle 26, -2, 20 \rangle \]
\[ |\vec{AB} \times \vec{AC}| = \sqrt{26^2 + (-2)^2 + 20^2} = \sqrt{676 + 4 + 400} = \sqrt{1080} \]
\[ \text{Luas segitiga} = \frac{1}{2} \cdot \sqrt{1080} = \boxed{\frac{\sqrt{1080}}{2} \approx 16.43} \]
